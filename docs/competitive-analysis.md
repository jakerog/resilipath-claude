## 1. Market Overview

The global Business Continuity Management (BCM) software market was valued at approximately **$1.4 billion in 2024** and is projected to reach **$3.2 billion by 2030** (CAGR ~14%). Key growth drivers:

- Increasing frequency and severity of cyberattacks (especially ransomware)
- Climate-related business disruptions (hurricanes, floods, wildfires)
- Regulatory pressure (SEC cyber disclosure rules, DORA in EU, HIPAA, FDA)
- Post-COVID recognition that most BCPs were inadequate
- Cloud adoption making geographically distributed DR exercises more common

## 2. Competitor Landscape

### Tier 1 — Enterprise BCM Platforms (Expensive, Complex)

#### Fusion Risk Management
- **Target:** Large enterprise (1,000+ employees)
- **Strengths:** Comprehensive, ISO 22301 aligned, strong GRC integration, Salesforce-native
- **Weaknesses:** Extremely expensive ($100K+/year), steep learning curve, implementation takes months, not designed for real-time exercise execution
- **Our advantage:** ResilienceOS is purpose-built for exercise execution (real-time task board, predecessor enforcement); much easier to implement; fraction of the cost

#### Archer (RSA / OpenPages)
- **Target:** Enterprise GRC
- **Strengths:** Market leader in GRC, deep compliance frameworks
- **Weaknesses:** BCM is an add-on module to a GRC platform; not its core strength; expensive; poor UX
- **Our advantage:** BCM is our core; UX is the product; we out-execute on real-time exercise management

#### ServiceNow BCM
- **Target:** Enterprise ServiceNow customers
- **Strengths:** Native integration with ITSM, change management, incident management
- **Weaknesses:** Only useful if you're already a ServiceNow enterprise customer; expensive per-seat model; BCM module is not the core product focus
- **Our advantage:** Standalone; integrate WITH ServiceNow rather than requiring it; purpose-built BCM UX

### Tier 2 — Mid-Market BCM Platforms

#### Castellan (formerly RecoveryPlanner)
- **Target:** Mid-market to enterprise
- **Strengths:** Purpose-built BCM, reasonable pricing, good BIA engine
- **Weaknesses:** Dated UX; no real-time exercise execution; primarily a document management tool with limited collaboration
- **Our advantage:** Real-time exercise management; modern mobile-first UX; resource check-in; live task board

#### Preparis
- **Target:** Mid-market
- **Strengths:** Good crisis communications, notification capabilities
- **Weaknesses:** Weaker on DR exercise specifics; BCM planning tools are basic
- **Our advantage:** DR Exercise Manager depth; BCP interview-driven generation; integrated workflow from plan → exercise → report

#### Avalution (now part of Sungard)
- **Target:** Professional services / consulting-delivered BCM
- **Strengths:** Consulting + software combination
- **Weaknesses:** Services-dependent model; not a true SaaS product
- **Our advantage:** Pure SaaS; self-service; scalable without consulting dependency

### Tier 3 — Point Solutions and Notification Tools

#### Everbridge
- **Target:** Enterprise mass notification / crisis communications
- **Strengths:** Best-in-class mass notification, global reach, regulatory notification intelligence
- **Weaknesses:** Not a BCM platform; no DR exercise management; no BCP planning
- **Our positioning:** Integrate with Everbridge for customers who already have it; compete directly with Module 3 for new customers

#### AlertMedia / Rave Mobile Safety
- **Target:** Employee notification during emergencies
- **Strengths:** Simple, fast mass SMS/push notification
- **Weaknesses:** Purely notification; no BCM planning, no DR exercise management
- **Our positioning:** Module 3 competes directly for mid-market; integrate for customers who already use them

## 4. Our Unique Value Propositions

### UVP #1: "The Only Platform Built for the Live Exercise Moment"
Every competitor treats exercise management as a checklist or document. ResilienceOS treats it as a live operational mission. Real-time task boards, predecessor enforcement, Go/No-Go gates, rollback activation, and evidence capture — all designed for the moment when the pressure is highest.

### UVP #2: "Replace Your Excel Runbook in 48 Hours"
A customer can import their existing Excel runbook, assign resources, schedule the exercise, and run it in the platform — all within 48 hours of signing up. No implementation consultants required.

### UVP #3: "Resource Availability — Solved"
The hardest problem in DR exercise management is knowing who will actually show up. ResilienceOS's Resource Check-In feature with automated escalations solves this before the exercise begins.

### UVP #4: "From Exercise to Document to Certificate"
ResilienceOS spans the entire BCM lifecycle: plan your BCP → run your DR exercise → generate evidence → demonstrate compliance → improve. No other mid-market product does all of this in one place.

## 5. Initial Target Customer Profile

**Company Type:** Mid-market to large enterprise  
**Employees:** 500–50,000  
**Industry:** Any — initial sweet spots: Manufacturing, Financial Services, Healthcare IT, Logistics, Technology
