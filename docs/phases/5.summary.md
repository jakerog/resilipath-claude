# Phase 5 Summary — Platform Maturation, Scale & Advanced Modules

## Objective
Expand ResilienceOS into a comprehensive BCM platform through iterative quarterly releases. Each quarter delivers one or more new modules or major capability expansions to existing modules. This phase has no fixed end date — it is the platform's continuous growth engine.

## Q1 Release: Business Impact Analysis Engine (Full)
Expanding the MVP BIA from Phase 3 into a full-featured enterprise-grade BIA tool.

### New Capabilities
- **Automated Criticality Scoring**: Algorithm-driven process criticality scoring based on revenue impact, regulatory dependency, customer SLA commitments, and recovery complexity
- **Dependency Mapping**: Visual dependency graph showing which processes depend on which systems, vendors, and people
- **Financial Impact Modeling**: Per-hour and per-day financial impact of process disruption (revenue loss, contractual penalties, recovery costs)
- **RTO/RPO Gap Analysis**: Compare current recovery capability against target RTO/RPO for each process; surface gaps
- **BIA Versioning**: Track how the organization's BIA evolves over time as the business changes
- **BIA Workshop Mode**: Facilitated BIA session mode where multiple stakeholders contribute answers in real-time
- **Executive BIA Summary**: One-page executive summary with risk heat map, top 5 critical processes, top 5 highest-impact risks

## Q2 Release: Vendor & Supply Chain Resilience
- **Vendor Risk Registry**: Tier vendors by criticality and concentration risk
- **Alternate Vendor Database**: Pre-mapped alternate suppliers for critical materials/services
- **Vendor Continuity SLAs**: Track vendor-committed RTO/RPO for key services
- **Supply Chain Disruption Simulation**: Model impact of losing a specific vendor on downstream operations
- **Vendor Portal**: Self-service portal where vendors submit their own BCP documentation and continuity commitments
- **Concentration Risk Report**: Identify single-vendor dependencies and geographic concentration risks

## Q3 Release: Regulatory & Compliance Tracker
- **Framework Library**: ISO 22301, NIST SP 800-34, DRII GPG, BCI Good Practice Guidelines, FFIEC, NERC CIP (energy), HIPAA Security Rule, SOX, PCI-DSS
- **Maturity Assessment**: Questionnaire-driven maturity scoring against each framework (1-5 scale)
- **Gap Register**: Identified gaps with owner assignment, remediation plan, target date, status
- **Evidence Locker**: Attach evidence documents to compliance requirements
- **Audit-Ready Report**: Generate framework-aligned audit packages with all evidence attached
- **Regulatory Calendar**: Upcoming regulatory filing deadlines and submission tracking

## Q4 Release: Incident & Event Manager (Full)
Expanding the incident declaration from Phase 4 into a full incident management platform.

- **Incident Response Playbooks**: Step-by-step response procedures triggered by incident type
- **Incident Command Structure**: Digital Incident Command System (ICS) role assignment and coordination
- **Resource Deployment Tracker**: Track which people and assets are deployed to the incident response
- **Situation Report (SitRep) Generator**: Auto-generate formatted situation reports at defined intervals
- **After-Action Review (AAR) Module**: Full AAR facilitation, findings capture, corrective action tracking
- **Incident Cost Tracker**: Log costs incurred during incident response for insurance claims and financial reporting
